🍢 Malai Boti Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Boneless Chicken — 1 kg
  • Yogurt — 1 cup
  • Fresh Cream — 1/2 cup
  • Ginger Garlic Paste — 1 tbsp
  • Green Chili Paste — 2 tbsp
  • Black Pepper — 1 tsp
  • Salt — to taste
  • Lemon Juice — 2 tbsp

How to Make:

  1. Mix yogurt, cream, and all spices together.
  2. Add chicken and marinate for 1–2 hours.
  3. Place chicken on skewers.
  4. Cook in oven or grill for 20–25 minutes.
  5. Brush with butter and serve hot.
