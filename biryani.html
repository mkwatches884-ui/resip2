🍛 Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chicken – 1kg
  • Basmati Rice – 500g
  • Onion – 3
  • Tomatoes – 2
  • Yogurt – 1 cup
  • Ginger Garlic Paste – 2 tbsp
  • Biryani Masala – 2 tbsp
  • Green Chilies – 3
  • Oil – 1 cup
  • Salt as needed

Method:

  1. Wash rice and soak for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add chicken, tomatoes, yogurt and spices.
  5. Cook chicken for 15–20 minutes.
  6. Boil rice until 70% cooked.
  7. Layer chicken and rice.
  8. Add fried onions and cook on low flame for 20 minutes.

Enjoy your delicious Biryani! 😋
